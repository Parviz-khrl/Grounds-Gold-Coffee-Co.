Grounds & Gold

Coffee and a Cause

Grounds and Gold is a local café serving gourmet coffee, homemade baked goods and made from-scratch dishes. G&G is built on the foundation of fostering community through providing a warm atmosphere where family and friends can gather; as well as, providing community outreach through “Heart of Gold” notes and donations to local charities.
